Lemonade

Description:

When life gives you lemons, make the Best Lemonade Ever! This aptly named recipe is as good as it gets:
Sweet, tart, easy to throw together, and oh-so refreshing. Sweeten your day with our top-rated lemonade
recipe (and get our best lemonade serving and storage tips).

Ingridients

  • ⅓ Cups of white sugar
  • 1 Cup water
  • 9 Medium lemons, or more as needed
  • 7 Cups ice-cold water
  • Ice as needed
